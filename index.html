The Ocean Course and Lagoon Course each feature a distinct seaside links atmosphere. Meandering lagoons, undulating fairways, elevated Bermuda greens protected by steep-faced bunkers and a prevailing sea breeze create a challenging but compelling course that will require all 14 clubs to conquer. While both courses are located at the Inn & Club, guests at our sister resort, The Lodge & Club Ponte Vedra Beach, can always enjoy playing privileges and convenient access by shuttle.
